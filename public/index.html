About the practice

A small independent practice in Tunbridge Wells.

One surveyor, working with homeowners, landlords and small developers across Kent, East Sussex, South East London and the wider South East. Every report I deliver is one I’ve walked, measured, written and signed — no juniors, no template-and-tweak, no white-label sub-contracting.

Fees are fixed and agreed up front. Reports are written in plain English. The post-survey call to talk through the findings is included.

Services

Four lines of work, all delivered personally.

Pick the one you need, or get in touch if you’re not sure — we can talk it through.

01 / Surveys

Building surveys

RICS-style condition reports for buyers, sellers and owners. Roof to drains, illustrated, written in plain English. The right level of survey for the property in front of you.

02 / EPCs

Energy certificates

Domestic Energy Performance Certificates lodged on the central register. Required for sale, let and most lender applications. Booked, surveyed and lodged inside 48 hours where possible.

03 / Retrofit

Retrofit assessments

PAS 2035-compliant assessments for ECO grants, MEES exemptions and self-funded retrofit. Fabric-first, building-specific, written so the design team and the homeowner can both work from it.
